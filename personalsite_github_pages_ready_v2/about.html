About Me

Get to know my journey, passions, and aspirations

My Journey

I'm currently pursuing my Master of Science in Information Systems at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, with a focus on cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and SAP. My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Science in Business, majoring in Information Systems with a co-major in Business Analytics.

What drives me is the intersection of technology and business impact. I believe that data, when properly analyzed and presented, can transform organizations and create meaningful change. My experience at AlgoLeap Technologies taught me the importance of not just building solutions, but building solutions that matter.

My Passions

Beyond academics and professional work, I'm passionate about mentorship and giving back to the community. As a mentor for elementary students and a peer tutor, I've discovered that teaching others not only helps them grow but also deepens my own understanding.

I'm also a certified CPR trainer, a brown belt karate champion, and I hold a Guinness World Record! These achievements reflect my commitment to continuous learning, physical discipline, and pushing boundaries.
